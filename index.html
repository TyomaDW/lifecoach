About Me

About Me

Hello! I'm Natalie Cruz, a passionate life coach from Bogota Colombia, speaking both Spanish and English, dedicated to helping individuals like you find clarity, purpose, and fulfillment in life. With a background in business, education, counseling and personal development, I have spent the past 10 years guiding clients through transformative journeys of self-discovery and growth.

I believe that everyone has the potential to lead a fulfilling and meaningful life, and my mission is to empower you to unlock that potential. My coaching approach is personalized, compassionate, and goal-oriented. I combine proven strategies with a deep understanding of the human experience to help you overcome obstacles, set and achieve meaningful goals, and create a life you truly love.

My Philosophy

I believe that every person is unique, and there is no one-size-fits-all approach to personal development. That’s why I take the time to understand your unique needs, challenges, and aspirations. Whether you're looking to improve your relationships, advance your career, or find more balance and joy in your everyday life, I am here to support you every step of the way.

My Approach

My coaching sessions are designed to be a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and desires.
I use a variety of tools and techniques, including Co-creation between coach & client, Coach helps clients find their own answers, to help you gain insights, develop new skills, and take
actionable steps toward your goals, connect with the Life Coach Approach through one on one coaching sessions, workshops, inspirational talks, and NLP technique.

Why Work With Me?

  • Experienced Guidance: With years of experience and numerous success stories, I have a proven track record of helping clients achieve meaningful change.
  • Compassionate Support: I provide a supportive environment where you feel heard, understood, and empowered to take control of your life.
  • Personalized Coaching: I tailor my coaching sessions to your specific needs, ensuring that you get the most out of our time together.
  • Efficient cultural transitions and identity development for expatriates or anyone adjusting to a new country, a new city, a new home.
